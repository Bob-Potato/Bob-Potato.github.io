The Coral Reef.

A Coral Reefs is "a ridge of rock in the sea formed by growth and deposit of coral" The coral reef is on of the most diverse and beautiful ecosystems in the world with thousands of different types of fish and coral.

Human Uses.

Humans have some uses for the coral reefs.

Tourism

Because the coral reef is such a beautiful place many tourist visit and can make humans a lot of money.

Fishing

In the reefs where fishing is allowed ( and sometimes in the ares where it's not) people can go fishing to catch a variety of different fish.

Wave protection

Undewater coral reef are very good at stopping waves from crashing into the shore. This has often prevented human buiding being damaged and may have even saved lives

Human Threats.

The coral reef are some of the most beautiful ecosystems in the world but unfortunately they are being threatened by the actions of humans.

There are some examples below.

Oil Spills

When oil is spilled it can spread to the reefs and kill many of the fish and the coral .

Naughty Tourists

Sometimes when divers or snorkelers visit the reef they can accidentaly hurt or sometimes even take pieces of the reef with them!.

Bleached Coral

When coral is exposed to too much heat or pollution the algae living on the coral leaves. When this happens the coral turns white and eventually dies.
